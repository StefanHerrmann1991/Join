Help

Join is a drag and drop kanban board. It is used for project management and task organization. With this type of board, you can visually represent the different stages of a project or workflow, and then drag and drop tasks between those stages as they are completed.

  • Join consists of several columns representing different stages of a project, such as "To Do," "In Progress,", "Awaiting Feedback" and "Done".
  • You can create a new task by clicking on the "Add Task" selector in the menu or by simply adding tasks at the board using the plus symbol or the "Add Task"-Button.
  • Drag and drop tasks between columns: Once you have tasks on your board, you can easily move them between columns as their status changes. To do this, simply click and hold the task you want to move, and then drag it to the column where you want it to go.
  • Edit task details: You can edit task details by clicking on the task card. You can change the task name, add a description, assign a due date, assign users or add subtasks to help organize your board.
  • Archive completed tasks: When a task is completed, you can archive it to keep your board clean and organized. Simply click on the task card and move it to the "Done" board.
  • Collaborate with others: If you're working on a project with others, you can invite them to view and edit your kanban board. This allows everyone to stay on the same page and keep track of progress in real-time.

That's it! With these steps, you should be able to create and use a drag and drop Join to manage your projects and tasks effectively.
